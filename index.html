1. About Google

Google is a global technology leader best known for its powerful search engine, innovative products, and commitment to making information universally accessible. Founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, Google has expanded far beyond search, offering services like Gmail, Google Maps, YouTube, and the Android operating system. The company continues to invest in artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and other emerging technologies to enhance user experiences and solve complex problems around the world.

2. Google’s Impact on Daily Life

From finding quick answers to navigating new cities, Google has become an essential part of everyday life for billions of people. Its search engine processes over 8.5 billion queries per day, while tools like Google Drive, Google Photos, and Google Meet help individuals and businesses stay connected and productive. With a focus on innovation and accessibility, Google continues to shape the way we learn, communicate, and explore the world around us
